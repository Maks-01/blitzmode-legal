BlitzMode Support

Need help with BlitzMode? You're in the right place.

Contact

Email: support@blitzmodeapp.com

We typically reply within 1–3 business days.

What to include in your message

  • Device model and iOS version
  • BlitzMode app version
  • Short description of the issue (what you expected vs. what happened)
  • Screenshots or screen recording (if applicable)

Tip: If the issue involves a file (PDF/EPUB/TXT), tell us the file type and approximate size.

Common questions

My subscription / Pro features didn’t activate.
Please make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID used for purchase. Then try:
1) Close and reopen the app
2) Check your internet connection
3) Use Restore Purchases (if available in the app)

The app crashes or freezes.
Restart the app and your device. If it still happens, email us with steps to reproduce.

How do I request a feature?
Send your idea to the support email — we read everything and prioritize based on impact.
