Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

Overview

BlitzMode respects your privacy. This app is designed to work primarily offline and does not collect, store, or sell personal data.

Data Collection

BlitzMode does not collect personal information such as:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Device identifiers for tracking

The app may process limited technical data strictly required for functionality:

  • Subscription status information provided securely by Apple via StoreKit
  • Anonymous usage analytics (only if enabled in future versions)

Subscriptions & Payments

All subscriptions and payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. BlitzMode does not receive, store, or process payment information.

Local Data Storage

Reading progress, imported files, and app settings are stored locally on your device. BlitzMode does not upload this information to external servers.

If cloud sync features are introduced in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Third‑Party Services

BlitzMode relies solely on Apple services (such as StoreKit and the App Store) for subscription validation and distribution.

Data Security

BlitzMode uses Apple’s secure system frameworks and does not transmit sensitive user data to external servers.

Children’s Privacy

BlitzMode does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a revised effective date on this page.
